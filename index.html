What is Leverage?

LeverageVIP is an invite-only community of founders and CEOs from the proptech industry that offers peer-support to grow and thrive your proptech business.

In business, the power of mentors are overrated and the power of peers are underrated. In Leverage, each member commits to bring their superpowers to add meaningful value to other members. Running your business is a lonely job and having a safe space to openly share your challenges and get support is extremely valuable.

The best way to overcome the challenges is to learn from the wins and mistakes of others, and to surround yourself with founders who’ve been there, done that.

Being part of leverage.VIP significantly increases the probability of your business growth. Access to small, confidential peer networks that can play a crucial role in an executive’s professional success. The greatest benefit these groups offer are peer advisory forums where members can candidly discuss ideas, strategies, and challenges,

Leverage helps foster long-term relationships. Meeting two new people and agreeing to meet each quarter is far more rewarding than meeting 10 new people at a conference and never speaking again.
